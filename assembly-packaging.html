Integrated Medical Operations

Contamination-Controlled Device Assembly & Heat Sealing

At Vidhata Plastics, moulding is only the first step. Our dedicated ISO Class 7 (Class 10,000) cleanroom assembly suites provide complete contract manufacturing services for complex medical devices, IV administration sets, catheter hubs, cassette bodies, and diagnostic disposable kits.

Equipped with 20kHz / 35kHz ultrasonic welding stations, micro-solvent bonding fixtures, 100% inline pressure decay leak detectors, and temperature/pressure validated heat sealers, our trained technicians process components from raw moulding to final sterile barrier packaging without ever breaking cleanroom protocol.

Sterile Packaging Excellence

ISO 11607 Validated Sterile Barrier Packaging

Sterility preservation requires rigorous thermal packaging controls. Vidhata operates digital impulse heat sealers with real-time monitoring of seal temperature, dwell time, and sealing pressure to ensure peel strength and seal integrity across every pouch.

Our packaging processes accommodate medical-grade Tyvek® / Poly pouches, peelable foil laminates, and rigid thermoformed trays. All sealed packages undergo dye penetration testing, peel strength testing (ASTM F88), and burst pressure testing (ASTM F2054) to ensure seamless compatibility with Ethylene Oxide (ETO), Gamma Radiation, and E-Beam sterilization methods.

Sterile Barrier Sealing
Impulse Heat Sealing · ISO 11607 Validation · ETO / E-Beam Ready
